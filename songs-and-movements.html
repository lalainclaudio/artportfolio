Songs and Movements

Musical Instruments and Games

" Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything." 
            - Plato

Papercup phone

These are good instrument to motivate children to listen and talk with each other alternately. 

Homemade drum set with stick

This set was made from reusable containers, tissue holder, tin cans, and bottle covers to create different sounds. The children can observe, listen, bang the stick soft or hard to create different sounds.

Homemade shakers

These homemade shakers are made from reusable small bottles are good instruments for children with small hands. They are good addition to any kind of music being sung or played. The children can play the shakers along while singing or listening to music as well as dance with it.

Twanger

Made from tin can, elastic bands and a tissue holder. This instrument can be used as an entry level before ukulele or guitar. Children can play with the twanger to create their own music. The elastic bands made the twanger easier to play.

Scarf

Scarves are good to use for music with dance movements, they are light and easy to use for children. Different movements can be made as well as simply playing with it.

Game

Instructions: 
    1. The requirement of this game is to have two children to do the game.
      2. Give each child a pice of paper.
      3. Ask the children to fold the paper crosswise to make a half sheet.
      4. On the first half ask the children to draw the Head until half of the body of their favourite animal.
      5. Ask the children to give exchange their papers without showing what they drew.
      6. Ask the children to draw the other have of the body of their favourite animal.
      7. Return the paper to the original owner and open.
