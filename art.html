Collage

Open-ended and process based activity that enhances children's fine motor skills and encourages children's awareness of different colours.

     Materials: coloured of papers, non-toxic acrylic paints, non-toxic water colours, brushes, toothbrushes, cottons, pencils and coloured pens. 

Textile

Open-ended and process based activity wherein children were presented with different materials. Children will improve hand and eye coordination, creativity and learn different textures from different materials.

 Materials: different types of coloured and printed cloths, tube cleaners, felt paper, sticks, yarn, and glue 

Sculpture

Process based activity. This activity will enhance children's fine motor skills, learn different textures and learn different coulours.

Materials: salt play dough, clay, papers of different colours, clothes pin, buttons, different textiles

Paint

 Open-ended and process based activity using materials presented to children. Enhances children's sensory development and fine motor skills.
  Materials: non toxic acrylic paint, non-toxic water colours, brushes, coloured pens, 

Crafts

Open-ended and process based activity. Enhances children's hand and eye coordination, fine motor skills, knowledge of texture and colours.

Materials: coloured yarns, sticks, glue, wire, hanger

Reusable Material

Enhances the child's environmental awareness to re-use and recycle materials to make art. 
     Materials: carton boxes, styrofoam, egg cartons (if no allergy concerns)

Natural Material

 Open-ended and process base activity that will encourage children's awareness of texture, colours and develop fine motor skills.

     Materials : loose leaves, glue, coloured and printed papers, googly eyes, coloured yarn, coloured feather and textiles
